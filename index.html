A warm welcome to the website of diet Matrshyam Hisar. It goes without saying, that education, rather quality education, is and will be the basis of all life and activity in not only our country but also on our planet. The role of teacher is of permanent importance in the overall development of society in any and every country. It is with these objectives in mind that the faculty of D.I.E.T Matrshyam Hisar works in every venture that it undertakes. In addition, no stone is left unturned to provide the education and training through innovative and activity based and ICT governed methods and techniques.
